# Northwind — Case Study One-Pager

Send to FinTech, B2B SaaS, or any company where organic is the moat.

## Headline metric

**0 → 480K monthly organic sessions in 14 months.**

## The setup

- **Client:** Northwind (FinTech, B2B)
- **Engagement:** 14 months, SEO + content + analytics
- **Senior team:** 1 SEO lead, 1 content lead, 1 analyst
- **Goal at kickoff:** make organic the largest channel by month 12

## The problem

Northwind was invisible on category-defining search terms. Every signup was bought. CAC was 2.4× the industry benchmark, and the company had zero compounding asset. In-house team could ship one article a week. The agency before us promised SEO in 90 days and delivered 12 generic posts.

## What BAZ shipped

1. **Topical map around comparison and alternative queries.** Built a 480-keyword map organized by intent, sized by traffic potential and conversion likelihood.
2. **Programmatic SEO engine.** 1,200 templated pages — "[Competitor] alternative for [Industry]" — each unique, each with real content, each linked from the hub.
3. **Editorial calendar with senior writers.** 8 long-form pieces per month, each tied to a specific funnel stage, each cited by 2-3 industry publications.
4. **Server-side analytics.** Built a multi-touch attribution model that finally made CAC visible per channel. Organic showed up as 38% of pipeline by month 6, 51% by month 14.
