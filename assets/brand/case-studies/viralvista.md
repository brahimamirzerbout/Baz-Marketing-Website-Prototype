# ViralVista — Case Study One-Pager

Send after a discovery call with a DTC or e-commerce lead.

## Headline metric

**ROAS 1.8 → 4.6 in 90 days.**

## The setup

- **Client:** ViralVista (DTC beauty brand, $2M–$5M ARR range)
- **Engagement:** 90 days, performance + SEO + content
- **Senior team:** 1 paid lead, 1 SEO lead, 1 content lead (no juniors)
- **Forecast made at kickoff:** 3.0× ROAS in 90 days, with month 4 free if missed

## The problem

ViralVista was spending $80K/mo on Meta with a 1.8 ROAS and no compounding asset. Creative fatigue hit every 9 days. Branded search was an afterthought. The attribution model counted every post-iOS signal as a paid conversion, so the dashboard said "we're crushing it" while the bank account said otherwise.

## What BAZ shipped

1. **Creative matrix rebuilt around UGC.** Replaced studio shoots with a 14-creator pipeline, each with 3 hooks, refreshed weekly.
2. **Search engine to capture branded demand.** SEO content + paid brand defense made "viralvista" a moat, not a leak.
3. **Server-side attribution.** Stannded-up Stape + Conversions API + first-party signals. Stopped overcounting paid conversions.
4. **Lifecycle onboarding flow.** New 7-email sequence lifted activation from 41% to 67% in 30 days, LTV up 41%.
