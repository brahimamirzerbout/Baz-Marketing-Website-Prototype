# BAZ — Brand Brief

> The thinking behind every word, color, and interaction.

---

## One-line positioning

**BAZ is the boutique agency for brands that want to be remembered, not just seen.**

We make brands unignorable. From 0 to 100M views — we've done it.

## Voice

- **Confident, not cocky.** We state what we believe, then prove it.
- **Specific, not generic.** "Boutique" beats "world-class." "240 brands" beats "many clients."
- **Editorial, not corporate.** We write like a magazine, not a brochure.
- **Honest about constraints.** Senior team, no juniors, no bullshit.

## Hero promise

> **Brand & Buzz.**
> We make brands unignorable.

This is the spine. Every page references it, every section reinforces it.

## Five brand beliefs (used as section eyebrows)

1. **Brands are verbs, not nouns.** A brand is something a company *does*, not something it *has*.
2. **Distribution is design.** The best creative in the world dies if nobody sees it.
3. **Senior team, no juniors.** The people who pitch are the people who ship.
4. **Speed is a feature.** Markets don't wait. We ship in weeks, not quarters.
5. **Measure what matters.** Followers are vanity. Revenue is sanity.

## Service pillars (6)

1. **Brand & Identity** — Naming, logo, identity systems, voice
2. **Web & Product** — Marketing sites, e-commerce, SaaS frontends, design systems
3. **Performance Marketing** — Paid social, paid search, programmatic, CRO
4. **Content & Social** — Editorial, video, social strategy, influencer
5. **SEO & Analytics** — Technical SEO, content SEO, analytics, dashboards
6. **AI & Innovation** — LLM integrations, AI workflows, automation, RAG

## Process (5 phases)

1. **Discover** — Audit, research, opportunity mapping
2. **Define** — Strategy, positioning, message architecture
3. **Design** — Visual identity, UX, prototypes, copy
4. **Develop** — Build, integrate, ship, train
5. **Scale** — Optimize, iterate, compound

## Audience

Primary: **founders & CMOs of $2M–$50M ARR companies** who are tired of agencies that over-pitch and under-deliver. They want senior people, fast iteration, and proof.

Secondary: **in-house marketing leads** who need an external team for overflow or specialized work.

## Visual language

- **Type:** Editorial serif for display (Fraunces or PP Editorial New), geometric sans for body (Inter or Söhne)
- **Color:** Off-black `#0e0e0e`, paper `#f5f1ea`, accent red `#8a8a8a`, accent gold `#a0a0a0`
- **Motion:** Cinematic. Scroll-reveal. Custom cursor. Magnetic CTAs. Marquee.
- **Density:** Generous whitespace. Big type. Few words per screen.

## Editorial principles

- Show the work, not the pitch deck
- Numbers over adjectives ("240+ brands" not "many brands")
- Short sentences. Period.
- The agency has an opinion. State it.
