# BAZ Presentation Guide

Presentation slide templates using the BlackSwan Design System.

---

## Title Slide (1920×1080)

- **Background:** `#0e0e0e` (ink)
- **Overlay:** Mesh gradient (violet radial from center-top at 0.10 opacity)
- **Grid:** 40px lines at 0.03 opacity
- **Content:**
  - Eyebrow: JetBrains Mono 13px, violet, tracking 2.5px, `● SECTION LABEL`
  - Title: Fraunces 300, 96px, paper `#f5f1ea`
  - Subtitle: Inter 400, 22px, ink-500 `#737373`
  - Violet accent line: 160px wide, 2px tall
  - Ghost mark: Bottom-right at 0.05 opacity

---

## Content Slide (1920×1080)

- **Background:** `#f5f1ea` (paper)
- **Grid:** 40px lines at 0.03 opacity
- **Content:**
  - Eyebrow: JetBrains Mono 13px, violet, tracking 2.5px
  - Heading: Fraunces 400, 64px, ink-900
  - Body: Inter 400, 20px, ink-600 `#525252`
  - Bullet style: `•` prefix with 28px line height
  - Bottom rule: `#f0ebe0` 1px
  - Ghost mark: Bottom-right at 0.05 opacity
  - Page number: JetBrains Mono 10px, ink-400, right-aligned

---

## Dark Section Slide (1920×1080)

- **Background:** `#0e0e0e` (ink)
- **Overlay:** Mesh gradient at 0.08 opacity
- **Content:**
  - Quote or stat in Fraunces 300, 72px, paper
  - Attribution in Inter 400, 16px, ink-400
  - Violet accent line below quote

---

## Two-Column Slide (1920×1080)

- **Background:** `#f5f1ea` (paper)
- **Layout:** Split view — left 55% content, right 45% visual/data
- **Left:** Eyebrow + heading + bullets (same as content slide)
- **Right:** Image, chart, or data visualization with ink background

---

## CTA / Closing Slide (1920×1080)

- **Background:** `#0e0e0e` (ink)
- **Overlay:** Strong mesh gradient at 0.15 opacity
- **Content:**
  - Eyebrow: `● Ready?`
  - Headline: Fraunces 300, 88px, paper, centered
  - Violet CTA button: "Start your project →"
  - Mark: Centered below CTA

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Color | Tracking |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------|----------|
| Slide title | Fraunces | 64–96px | 300 | paper / ink-900 | -0.04em |
| Section heading | Fraunces | 48px | 400 | ink-900 | -0.03em |
| Body text | Inter | 20px | 400 | ink-600 | 0 |
| Eyebrow | JetBrains Mono | 13px | 400 | violet | 0.18em |
| Caption/meta | JetBrains Mono | 10px | 400 | ink-400 | 0.15em |
