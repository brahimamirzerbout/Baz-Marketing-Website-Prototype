# EngageEra — Case Study One-Pager

Send to any B2B SaaS with PMF but weak distribution.

## Headline metric

**Trial-to-paid 0.6% → 2.4% in 6 months. New MRR +318%.**

## The setup

- **Client:** EngageEra (B2B SaaS, post-launch, 12K free signups stalled)
- **Engagement:** 6 months, strategy + paid + brand + lifecycle + CRM
- **Senior team:** 1 growth lead, 1 brand lead, 1 lifecycle lead, 1 ops lead
- **Goal at kickoff:** lift trial-to-paid to 2.0% by month 6, hit $X ARR target

## The problem

Post-launch, EngageEra had 12K free signups and a 0.6% trial-to-paid conversion. The site wasn't built for product-led growth — it was a brochure. The paid channel was buying free signups that never converted. Sales had no idea which leads were worth working.

## What BAZ shipped

1. **PQL scoring on the home page.** Replaced "Sign up" with a 3-question context capture. New signups got a PQL score 0-100 from the first session.
2. **Lifecycle in Customer.io.** 5-email activation sequence, branched on PQL score. High-PQL went to sales in 4 hours.
3. **Paid + content engine.** Stopped buying signups. Started buying intent — comparison queries, integration searches, "alternative to" keywords.
4. **Brand and homepage rebuild.** Moved from "we built a cool product" to "here's why teams like yours switch to us in 30 days." Conversion rate on the homepage went from 1.2% to 3.8%.
